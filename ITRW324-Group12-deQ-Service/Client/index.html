Our Story

DeQ was started by a group of 6 developers in July 2018 in room G3e of the main North West University Library. In that room, several ideas were discussed and DeQ was born. The aim of DeQ was to minimize the amount of time spent in queues and instead utilise that time in a more productive manner.

DeQ's vision is a world free of queues where businesses can save massive amounts of money on rent and customers can save a large amount of time in queues.

Our most important resource is time, as Benjamin Franklin said, "Time is money". With our award winning virtual queueing system, you now have more time to spend with your families and doing the things you love, rather than in a long queue which leaves you feeling frustrated and violated.

At DeQ, nothing is more important to us than customer satisfaction, which is why we have developed a modern solution to the outdated practice of waiting. At the click of a button, you can be in the queue while still in bed or spending time with your loved ones.

With DeQ's amazing service, you will receive a notification on your phone when your turn is coming up giving you enough time to reach the business without wasting time.
